Code Master
The Annual Coding Challenge of SRKREC

Experience the ultimate test of spelling expertise at our Spell Bee Championship! In the first round, contestants will listen to a word and select the correct option from multiple choices. Students write out the words in the second round, their spelling abilities will be put down to the test. Finally, in the third round, contestants will spell the words out loud, showcasing their true mastery of language. Join us for a thrilling competition that will challenge and delight word enthusiasts."Unlock the power of words and compete against the best spellers in town at our Spell Bee Championship" - an extraordinary event that will leave you spellbound.Only SRKREC students (all branches & years of B.E/B.Tech programmes) are eligible to take part in this challenge,can win exciting prizes and e certificates.

Who can participate?

students from any branch and in any year are eligible to enter the competition..

Does the test have a fee?

The first level cost 20 rupees,and if the player wants to replay it more than once, he must spend 10 rupees each time.The second and third levels are free to play.

What is the advantage of participating in this contest?

The exicting prizes are just provided for motivation,greater than the awards. You may evaluate your speaking talents and see where you rank among SRKR's potential speakers. spell bee champ 2023 will provide a rank to top 20 students based on their overall performance in the competition.
